Services

We perform on a number of functions as listed below;

CHURCH SERVICES

Imagine your church service filled with the soul-stirring harmonies of the Carnelian Gents. Their uplifting gospel renditions of traditional and contemporary hymns will inspire your congregation and create a truly special atmosphere of worship.

WEDDINGS

Your wedding day deserves a soundtrack that reflects the love and joy of the occasion. The Carnelian Gents can provide a touch of elegance with their beautiful a cappella arrangements, perfect for both the ceremony and the reception. They can even personalize their performance to include special songs that hold meaning for you and your partner.

INTRODUCTION CEREMONIES

Okwangula/Kuhingira, the traditional Ugandan introduction ceremony, is a momentous occasion. The Carnelian Gents can add a special touch to this celebration with their vibrant a cappella melodies. Their uplifting music will set a joyful tone and create lasting memories for you and your families.

GRADUATIONS

Graduation is a time to celebrate achievement. The Carnelian Gents can create a celebratory atmosphere with their energetic gospel tunes, tailoring their message to the joy and accomplishment of this milestone.

BIRTHDAY PARTIES

Looking to make your birthday party unforgettable? The Carnelian Gents can bring the party alive with their upbeat gospel renditions of popular songs and classic party anthems. Their infectious energy is guaranteed to get everyone moving and singing along.

CONCERTS

Experience the full power of the Carnelian Gents' a cappella mastery at their live concerts. Their repertoire spans a range of gospel styles, from traditional hymns to contemporary pieces. Get ready for an evening of beautiful harmonies and uplifting music that will leave you feeling inspired.
