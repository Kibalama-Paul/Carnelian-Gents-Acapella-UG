Carnelian Gents is an Acapella Group that does Acapella music at a number of events like weddings, graduation parties, birthday parties,church services, concerts and many other Entertainment Events.

Carnelian Gents Acapella started in 2020 at Lakeside College Luzira as small Acapella Group that had big dreams of spreading the heavenly good news by the use of gospel music to this generation winning souls and bring them closer to Jesus christ as our personal saviour through baptism of Water immersion, as practiced by Seventh-day Adventists, symbolizing Christ's death and resurrection.

The world of acapella music is filled with talented groups, but few resonate with the soul quite like the Carnelian Gents. This Ugandans ensemble uses their voices alone to create a tapestry of rich gospel harmonies that uplift and inspire..

At the heart of the Carnelian Gents Acapella' sound are six phenomenal vocalists. Kibirige Jimmy brings a powerful lead that grounds the group's sound. Sseruwu Timothy's powerful bass adds an angelic touch, while Kibalama Paul's smooth baritone weaves through the melodies. Anchoring the harmonies are Aloch Chrispus that does beatboxing and the high Atlo and Wasswa Jonathan that does the soaring Tenor that also brings that heavenly touch on earth, their voices blending seamlessly to create a full and dynamic soundscape. And leading the group with spiritual conviction is Kirya Joseph who is A back-up lead vocalists that does those soft runs and ruffs making wonderful adlibs in our music, his passionate delivery tying the message of each song to the listener's heart.
